About Us

What Makes Our Coffee Special?

We buy only from reputable farmers and millers at volumes equal to several hundred acres. We grade our coffees into different sizes and quality, throw out the lower quality beans and blind taste our grades and types individually to find the best roasting temperature that bring out the best taste for that specific size and coffee bean – different roasting temperatures for different coffee. We do this constantly to maintain a consistent and high-quality roast every time.

Speciality coffee is quality driven — not like all the other coffees out there. You can pretty much guess at this point that your normal run of the mill coffee in your local grocer would not be considered speciality coffee.

Many coffee businesses are popping up around the world but most of these businesses are often misunderstood. You may not also know what makes specialty coffee special. So, we are not going to leave you in the dark, we’ll tell you many things you may not understand about this new wave of coffee.
